Getting Started

For New LSR Submissions:

  1. Click "Submit New Request" to access the LSR form
  2. Fill in administrative information (PON, CCNA, NNSP, etc.)
  3. Enter service location details
  4. Add phone numbers via text input or CSV upload
  5. Review and submit - PDF will be automatically generated

For Status Tracking:

  1. Click "Check Status" to access the tracking system
  2. Search by PON number or specific phone number
  3. View detailed status information and completion dates
  4. Track progress through carrier approval process
